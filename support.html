About Eagles18

App Name: Eagles18

Developer: Philipp Schmidbauer

Eagles18 is a comprehensive golf tracking and scoring app for iOS and Apple Watch. Track your rounds, analyze your performance with detailed statistics, and improve your game with real-time GPS tracking and advanced analytics.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking a round?

Open Eagles18, select your course, and tap "Start Round". The app will guide you through scoring and tracking your game.

Does Eagles18 work offline?

Yes! You can score rounds and track your game even without an internet connection. Your data will sync once you're back online.

How do I use the Apple Watch app?

Open Eagles18 on your Apple Watch to score directly from your wrist. You can track shots, view distances, and complete your scorecard without pulling out your phone.

How do subscriptions work?

Eagles18 offers monthly and yearly auto-renewing subscriptions. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your Apple ID settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, select Eagles18, and tap "Cancel Subscription".

Can I export my data?

No not yet, but we're working on this feature.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can request account deletion directly within the Eagles18 app under Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.
